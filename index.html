About Pressle Coin Elephant

Elephant Pow (ELP) - A Decentralized Token for the Future of Digital Finance Elephant (ELP) is an innovative cryptocurrency token designed to offer unique and fair investment opportunities within the blockchain ecosystem. With a total supply of 1 quadrillion, Elephant employs a meticulous distribution mechanism to ensure sustainable value and benefits for token holders..

Key Features

  • Unique Reward System: A distinctive reward system where 100% of the reward allocation is divided into two parts: 50% is allocated to the top 2000 holders, and 50% is distributed randomly to holders with at least 10 billion tokens, in varying amounts.
  • Burn Mechanism: With a 0.5% allocation for burning, Elephant periodically reduces the total supply of tokens to support scarcity and long-term value.
  • Elephant is committed to bringing innovation to the crypto space with a focus on sustainable development and real benefits for the community. We plan to evolve Elephant into a cross-chain blockchain and create a regulated centralized exchange (CEX) platform to ensure the security of customer funds and provide safe and reliable investment opportunities.
